**答案:** $-\frac{4}{3}$


**解析:** 解: $\because \theta$ 是第四象限角,

$\therefore-\frac{\pi}{2}+2 k \pi<\theta<2 k \pi$, 则 $-\frac{\pi}{4}+2 k \pi<\theta+\frac{\pi}{4}<\frac{\pi}{4}+2 k \pi, k \in Z$,
